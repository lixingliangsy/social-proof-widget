We want you to be completely satisfied with Social Proof Widget. If you're not happy with our service, we offer a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase.

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request the refund within 30 days of your initial subscription purchase

2. What's Covered

  • First-time subscriptions: Full refund within 30 days
  • Plan upgrades: Pro-rated refund for the unused portion
  • Accidental purchases: Full refund if reported within 48 hours

3. What's NOT Covered

  • Renewals: Automatic renewal charges are non-refundable unless canceled before the renewal date

  • Free plans: No payment = no refund
  • Violation of terms: Accounts terminated for policy violations are not eligible for refunds

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Email us at support@social-proof-widget.com with the subject "Refund Request"

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days (depending on your bank/payment provider)

⚠️ Important Note
Refunds are only available for subscriptions purchased directly through our website. If you purchased through a third-party platform (e.g., AppSumo, Gumroad), please refer to their refund policy.

6. Cancellation vs. Refund

  • Cancellation: You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger a refund for the current billing period.
  • Refund: A refund returns your payment and cancels the subscription.

Example: If you cancel on day 15 of your subscription, you can still use the service until day 30 (end of billing period), but you won't get a refund. If you request a refund on day 15, you get your money back and lose access immediately.

7. Exceptions

We may, at our discretion, offer refunds outside the 30-day window in exceptional circumstances, such as:

  • Billing errors on our part
  • Duplicate charges

These exceptions are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact support with details.
